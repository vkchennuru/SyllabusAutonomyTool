📖 About This Tool

The Syllabus Autonomy Assistant for PRE-BOS is a free, open-source tool designed for college faculty managing syllabus revisions under autonomous university frameworks (e.g., APSCHE). It automates syllabus enhancement, compliance checks, and outcome mapping—streamlining the Board of Studies (BOS) approval process while maintaining academic rigor.

Built as Open Educational Resource (OER) under CC BY 4.0, this tool is free for all faculty and institutions to use, modify, and redistribute.

🎯 Design Philosophy

Simplicity first: No complex configurations. Upload → Select → Generate.
Context-aware: Subject-wise intelligence for programming, theory, labs, commerce, and humanities.
Compliance-driven: Outputs designed for NAAC, IQAC, and BOS documentation.
Faculty-centered: Built by faculty, for faculty. Respects your time and expertise.
